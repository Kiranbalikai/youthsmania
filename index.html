About YouthsMania

As the legacy of "KLE's BCA Huballi" continues, the PU college fest "YOUTHSMANIA" is hosted and organized by the BCA students for PU students on ___ and ____ . This event is basically conducted to enhance and boost general knowledge with their Creativity & Fun. This forum focuses to develop the overall skills among the students This event provides the platform for students to exhibit and develop their talents.

Brief Info:
The event is "YOUTHSMANIA " followed by other sub-events.. Those are :

  • Master Mind (Quiz)
  • Shutter Bug (Photography)
  • Tech Art (Technical Art)
  • Gamezvilla (Gaming)
  • Verbattle (Communication)
  • Best Captain (All-Rounder)

The battle is between social and academic environment on two days where victorious one will be crowned with The Best All-Rounder.
